Beginner Level (A1 - A2)

  • Daily routines and habits
  • Third person singular rules
  • Common time expressions
03

Numbers & Time

  • Cardinal and ordinal numbers
  • Telling time and dates
  • Days, months, and seasons
  • Money and prices
04

Family & Relationships

  • Family vocabulary
  • Possessive adjectives
  • Describing people (appearance)
  • Simple conversations about family
05

Food & Dining

  • Food and drink vocabulary
  • Ordering at restaurants
  • Countable and uncountable nouns
  • Expressing likes and dislikes
06

Places in a City

  • Common places vocabulary
  • Asking for and giving directions
  • Prepositions of place
  • Transportation vocabulary

Expand your skills and gain confidence in everyday communication.

01

Past Tenses

  • Simple Past vs. Past Continuous
  • Used to and Would
  • Past Perfect introduction
  • Telling stories and anecdotes
02

Future Forms

  • Will vs. Going to
  • Present Continuous for future
  • Predictions and plans
  • Future time clauses
03

Modal Verbs

  • Ability, permission, and requests
  • Obligation and prohibition
  • Advice and suggestions
  • Deductions and speculations
04

Conditionals

  • Zero Conditional (facts)
  • First Conditional (real possibilities)
  • Second Conditional (hypothetical)
  • Unless, as long as, provided that
05

Reported Speech

  • Reporting statements
  • Reporting questions
  • Reporting commands and requests
  • Tense backshifting rules
06

Phrasal Verbs

  • Common phrasal verbs (A-Z)
  • Separable vs. inseparable
  • Phrasal verbs in context
  • Practice exercises and quizzes
